About Save tree :

Trees are the best gift of nature to the human being. We can’t ignore the importance of trees. Trees are very necessary for this planet to survive. That’s why it is said that save trees save life. Trees serve as the best friend of the human being. Trees provide us with oxygen and absorb carbon dioxide from the environment. It also controls the environmental pollution. Trees are the source of medicine, food for us. It also helps us in making our houses, furniture etc. We need to plant more trees to enjoy the benefits of trees.
